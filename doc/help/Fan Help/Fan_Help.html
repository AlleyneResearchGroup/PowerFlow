Thermal Management Systems

Description

The function of a fan in the ECS is to deliver the recirculated air from the cabin to a mixing junction, where the recirculated air is mixed with the conditioned air from the ECS. The fan model in Simulink determines the recirculated air properties and the fan power required.

Mathematical Model

The fan model is based on two performance curves. The input to the curves is the volume flow rate in cfm, from which the static pressure and the fan power is determined.
