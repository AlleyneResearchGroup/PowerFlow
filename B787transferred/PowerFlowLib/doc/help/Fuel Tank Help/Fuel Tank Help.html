Thermal Management Systems

Description

The fuel tank model tracks the time-varying fuel temperature, ullage temperature, and fuel mass. Mathematical modeling of the fuel tank is based upon conservation of energy and mass. In version 1.0, time-dependent pressure states are not calculated. Heat transfer is captured between the fuel, ullage, tank walls, and internal heat loads.
